ABOUT OUR LIBRARY

Welcome to CBIT Library

Our Mission

The CBIT Library is dedicated to providing students and faculty with comprehensive resources for academic excellence and research. We aim to create an environment that fosters learning, innovation, and intellectual growth across all engineering disciplines.

Library Overview

Our state-of-the-art library houses over 50,000 books covering various engineering disciplines including Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and more. The library subscribes to numerous international journals and provides access to digital resources that support the academic curriculum.

Facilities

  • Spacious reading halls with seating capacity for 300 students
  • Digital resource center with high-speed internet access
  • Research sections dedicated to specialized fields
  • Group discussion rooms for collaborative projects
  • 24/7 online catalog access
